# The Flayer's Workshop
The stench of decay fills this circular chamber where strips of flesh hang from rusted hooks, swaying gently in a nonexistent breeze. Dark stains mar the flagstone floor, forming intricate spiraling patterns that lead to a gore-encrusted wooden workbench. Flickering torchlight casts dancing shadows on the walls, where countless knives and flensing tools are mounted in grotesque displays.
**Enemy**: The Skinless Artisan - A spindly humanoid wearing an apron made of stitched faces
**Treasure**: The Crimson Needle - A magical bone needle that can sew flesh into armor

---

# The Purulent Archive
Shelves of rotting books line the walls of this library, their pages bloated and dripping with foul-smelling moisture. Black mold creeps across the ceiling in pulsing veins, occasionally releasing clouds of spores that glow with an sickly green light. The remains of previous visitors lie slumped over reading tables, their bodies serving as fertilizer for clusters of luminescent fungi.
**Enemy**: The Librarian of Decay - A fungal colony that has taken the shape of a robed scholar
**Treasure**: Tome of Toxic Truths - A spellbook that teaches poisonous magic

---

# The Digester's Den
Pools of bubbling acid dot this cavern's floor, releasing caustic vapors that sting the eyes and burn the throat. The skeletal remains of previous meals litter the ground, picked clean by both acid and teeth until only blackened bones remain. Along one wall, a collection of partially dissolved weapons and armor pieces forms a macabre gallery.
**Enemy**: The Acid Maw - A massive slug-like creature with multiple mouths
**Treasure**: The Caustic Crown - A corroded crown that grants acid resistance

---

# The Bloodmist Corridor
A perpetual crimson fog fills this long hallway, thick enough to muffle sounds and disorient travelers. The walls pulse rhythmically like living flesh, with occasional protrusions that resemble twisted faces locked in silent screams. Warm droplets fall from the ceiling, leaving red trails down your armor.
**Enemy**: The Mist Butcher - A barely visible humanoid that stalks through the red fog
**Treasure**: The Heart of the Mist - A beating organ that grants enhanced vision in fog

---

The Bone Sculptor's Gallery
Hundreds of skeletal sculptures fill this vast chamber, each one created from the bones of multiple creatures fused together in impossible combinations. The floor is carpeted with bone dust that crunches underfoot, and the air carries the acrid smell of marrow and grave dirt. In the center stands a massive work-in-progress, its empty eye sockets seeming to follow your movement.
**Enemy**: The Osseous Artist - A skeleton wearing a cloak of fused bones
**Treasure**: The Marrow Wand - A magical tool that can shape and fuse bone

---

# The Writhing Vault
The floor of this chamber writhes with countless serpents of various sizes, creating a living carpet of scales and fangs. Molted skins hang from the columns like grisly banners, some still bearing the faces of their former owners. The air is thick with the sound of slithering and the occasional rattle of warning.
**Enemy**: The Serpent Queen - A massive half-woman, half-snake creature
**Treasure**: The Venom Pearl - A black pearl that can store and release poison

---

# The Madness Echo Chamber
This octagonal room's walls are covered in mirrors that reflect impossible angles and spaces that shouldn't exist. Whispers emanate from the glass, speaking in languages that hurt the mind to comprehend. The floor appears to shift and tilt, though your sense of balance tells you you're standing still.
**Enemy**: The Mirror Witch - A being that exists simultaneously in all reflections
**Treasure**: The Paradox Prism - A mirror shard that can create temporary duplicates of items

---

# The Hunger Hall
Massive teeth jut from the walls of this flesh-lined corridor, snapping shut at random intervals with bone-crushing force. The floor is soft and yielding, like walking on a massive tongue, and digestive fluids occasionally drip from hidden glands in the ceiling. A wet, gurgling sound echoes through the passage with each movement.
**Enemy**: The Living Gut - A mass of digestive organs that has gained sentience
**Treasure**: The Glutton's Goblet - A chalice that converts anything placed inside into healing potions
