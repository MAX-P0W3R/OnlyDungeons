# Daily Dungeon: The Hidden Riches

**Embark on a thrilling solo dungeon adventure** where only the brave dare to venture deep into the heart of an ancient labyrinth. Beneath the stone and shadows, six menacing monsters guard untold riches—treasures that could make you the wealthiest adventurer to ever walk the realms. 

But beware! Each monster is more than just a challenge—they hold secrets, treasures, and one key that will unlock the path to the final, ultimate showdown. Time is against you: you have **30 rounds** to find these creatures, defeat them, and claim their loot before the dungeon consumes you.

**The clock is ticking...** Can you outsmart the monsters, find the elusive key, and conquer the final boss? Or will you fall short, your fortune slipping away like dust in the wind? The choice is yours, but the dungeon is unforgiving.

Good luck, adventurer. **May your wits be sharp and your sword sharper.**

---

## How to Play

You will navigate the dungeon's perilous corridors, searching for monsters, treasure, and the final boss lair. Time is running out, and every decision counts. Here are the key rules:

### Rules of the Dungeon:
- **30 rounds** to explore, battle, and loot the dungeon. Every second counts.
- **Find and defeat six powerful monsters**—each one hiding a stash of gold, potions, and treasures.
- **One of the monsters holds the key** to unlock the door to the Final Boss lair. Find it to proceed!
- **Conquer the Final Boss** or run out of time. Your fate lies in your hands.

---

## Available Commands:

- **'Look'**: Inspect your current surroundings. Who knows what hidden secrets you might uncover?
- **'Go [direction]'**: Venture deeper into the dungeon (North, South, East, West). Be cautious—danger lurks around every corner.
- **'Attack'**: Ready your weapon and engage in combat with any enemy in the room. Fight bravely!
- **'Get [item]'**: Pick up items (gold, potions, magical artifacts) when no enemy is nearby.
- **'Roll'**: Roll some dice for fun. You never know what lucky breaks might come your way.
- **'Help'**: Need guidance? Display the available commands.
- **'Rules'**: Review the game rules and get back on track.
- **'Exit'**: Ready to call it quits? Leave the dungeon (though you may regret it).
